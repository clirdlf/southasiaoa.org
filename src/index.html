South Asia Open Archives

Your gateway to a rich collection of open access materials for the study of South Asia.

The South Asia Open Archives (SAOA), a subset of the South Asia Materials Project (SAMP), creates and maintains a collection of open access materials for the study of South Asia. This initiative aims to enhance access to digital resources for South Asian studies globally.

Explore the SAOA Collection

Mission and Selection Criteria

SAOA addresses needs across all academic disciplines, focusing on colonial-era materials. Resources are selected based on research value, broad utility, uniqueness, risk factors, and complementarity to existing resources.
